Here you can add your custom tracking domains. We highly recommend adding a custom domain. The default voluumtrk.com is meant for testing purposes only.

In order to add a domain you will need to CNAME one of following domains: http://zx1jg.voluumtrk2.com, http://zx1jg.voluumtrk.com, http://zx1jg.trackvoluum.com. The main domain will be used to generate your campaign and click URLs. The backup domains allow you to quickly substitute your main domain in case it is compromised.
